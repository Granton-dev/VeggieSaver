🥬 The Problem

Households waste millions of tons of perfectly preventable food every year

Most vegetable spoilage isn't sudden — it's gradual. By the time you notice mould or slime, the vegetable has been deteriorating for days. Traditional solutions rely on expensive IoT sensors or manual inspection. VeggieGuard changes that with pure software intelligence.

⚠ Did you know? The average household throws away 30–40% of the food it purchases. Vegetables account for the largest share — most discarded simply because spoilage wasn't noticed in time.
